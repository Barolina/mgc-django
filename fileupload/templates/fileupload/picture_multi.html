Genre can be found in all forms of art, it is used to group various types of art according to some predictable range of features. Here our focus is to classifying music based on genre, which is a standard problem in Music Information Retrieval (MIR) research.

Music genres are often subjective and sometimes closely related. It will be a challenging task to automatically classify music into genres. Some music will be hard to classify into a particular genre because of its overlapping or multi-genre features.
